# Product Vision

> Product Layer — Long-term direction for AI Production Studio.

## 1 — Vision Statement

AI Production Studio is an agent-first development workspace that enables a solo founder to operate a full software production company using AI agents for planning, implementation, review, and delivery.

---

## 2 — Core Thesis

Software production can be decomposed into deterministic workflows where AI agents handle execution under founder supervision. The founder's role shifts from writing code to making decisions.

---

## 3 — Success Criteria

- A single founder can manage multiple concurrent projects.
- Every delivered artifact is traceable from client brief to production.
- Quality is maintained through automated review + founder approval gates.
- Cost is predictable and controlled through budget hierarchies.

---

## 4 — Non-Goals (v1)

- Multi-user collaboration
- Self-service client onboarding
- Fully autonomous operation (founder approval always required)
- Public marketplace for AI agents
