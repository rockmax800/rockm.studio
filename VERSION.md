# VERSION

## Current: v2.2 — Adaptive Intelligence

Released: 2026-03-24

### Summary

System-level AI evolution layer with formal self-modification (Gödel Mode), mutation experiments (Darwin Mode), cybernetic feedback loop, and Company Lead orchestration. Spatial Office rendering is always-on. Teams management fully integrated with capability-based assignment.

### What's New in v2.2

- **Company Lead** — AI Delivery Director as first point of contact for all new projects. Structured conversation with live scope extraction, internal team consultation simulation, and cost/token estimates before Blueprint creation
- **Gödel Mode** — formal self-modification proposals with constraint preservation proofs, evaluation rail integration, and mandatory founder approval
- **Darwin Mode** — controlled mutation experiments (prompt tweaks, trait shifts, stack changes) with baseline comparison, protected scenario gates, and promotion pipeline
- **Cybernetic Feedback Loop** — automated anomaly detection (rework rate, escalation frequency, CI failures, token inefficiency) generating correction proposals
- **Capability Evolution** — stability scoring, performance trend tracking, and high-performing capability cloning via CapabilityTemplate
- **Evolution Dashboard** — observability surface for all experiments, proposals, promotions, and rejected mutations at `/evolution`
- **Spatial Office** — always-on spatial map rendering capability rooms even when empty, with immediate reactivity on employee assignment
- **Mandatory Capability Assignment** — employee creation requires capability selection; immediate UI refresh across Teams, Office, and Capability views
